Future Plans

Like all video game developers, we have plans for future projects that we could realize. Unfortunately, most of these plans do not yet have a skeleton, let alone a detailed development and production plan. However, we can still share our thoughts and ideas with you. Most of our ideas and plans are focused on the FPS (First person shooter) format, based on horror and sci-fi elements. A multiplayer plan is also planned for some projects, but it is (for now) difficult to realize. For now, our developers are based exclusively on singleplayer ideas, due to the lack of budget for maintaining multiplayer games, although we are planning for the future as well.

As for the singleplayer plans, the sequel to the horror game "Pain Killer" is on the waiting list, which would be a much bigger project, in case Pain Killer manages to get the attention of players. In order not to combine too much, Pain Killer number two would contain more than twice as much story as the first part, and could also contain DLCs and multiplayer options. As for the story of the second part of Pain Killer, it should build on the original story, which players will find out when they play the video game, in order to preserve the surprise. In case you want to know the rough and tumble of Pain Killer, check out our projects.
