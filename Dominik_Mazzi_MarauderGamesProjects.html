Our Projects

Pain Killer

Our only active project(for now), called Pain Killer, is an action shooter, based on the story of a military scout who is sent to the planet Mars to a military facility with which Earth has lost all contact.

As time passes, the main character discovers bit by bit what it is about, that is, who/what is the cause of the problem, and that the hard way. Sneaking through the dark corridors, he realizes that something is watching him and lurking in the darkness. It's up to him to decide if he wants to know more, or just go for the main mission, which is to turn on the backup generators, re-establish contact with earth, and explore the base and eliminate threats in case they exist.
During the adventure, the pace will constantly and drastically change, which means that at some moments the player will have to sneak and hide, while at others he will have to keep a high pace in order to survive. The video game has been in development for a year, and it is not yet known when it will be perfected and released. A trailer will be released over the next year, so players can see what awaits them when the game is out. Until then, we've prepared just this for you, to prevent spoiling the enjoyment and experience that a video game should provide. In case you have questions or want to know more, contact us through Discord, and soon instagram and gmail will be available.
